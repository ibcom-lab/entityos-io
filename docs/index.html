What Is entityOS?

Designed to liberate.

A universal cloud operating system providing the objects, methods, identity, and automation infrastructure every entity needs — from a solo individual to a decentralised autonomous organisation.

Individual

Personal self-management and interaction with web and blockchain-based information services.

Organisation

Full information management and automation for contacts, projects, financials, and compliance.

Collective

Networks of organisations, individuals, and DAOs collaborating within a shared domain.

DAO

Information infrastructure for blockchain-based DAOs, supporting transactions and organisational compliance.

Security

A staged approach to enterprise security.

Practical guidance for securing entityOS deployments — from basic network controls to full cryptographic identity verification.

STAGE 01 — AVAILABLE

IP Allowlisting

Control who can reach your services at the network layer. Fast to deploy, immediately effective — the essential first boundary.

Read Guide
STAGE 02

mTLS + X.509

Layer cryptographic identity on top of network controls. Every connection proves who it is with a certificate — not just where it comes from.

Full Zero Trust
